Travel to Vail

The nearest large airport is Denver International Airport, from which it is about a 2 hour drive to get to Vail.

If you don't want to rent a car you may use the Epic Mountain Shuttle (operated by Vail Resorts) instead. Once you get to Vail there are several free shuttles and buses to help you navigate Vail Village and Lionshead Village.
