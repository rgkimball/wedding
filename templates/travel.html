Travel to Vail

The nearest large airport is Denver International Airport (DIA), from which it is about a 2 hour drive to get to Vail. Denver is a ~2 hour flight from Los Angeles or San Francisco, and about 4 hours west of the East Coast. Eagle Country Regional Airport (EGE) is a short 30 minute drive from Vail, though fewer commercial flights are available there. Once you arrive in Vail, a car will largely be unnecessary, so a van or shuttle from the airport may be a good alternative to renting a car.

If you decide not to rent a car you may use the Epic Mountain Shuttle (operated by Vail Resorts) to travel from DIA to Vail. Once you get to Vail there are several free shuttles and buses to help you navigate Vail Village and Lionshead Village, and our ceremony and reception are walkable from numerous hotels in both Lionshead and Vail Village.
